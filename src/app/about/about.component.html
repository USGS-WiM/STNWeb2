Ice Jams Frequently Asked Questions

Ice jams along rivers cause flooding, scouring, injuries and loss of life, and structural and environmental damage; they are a major hazard across the northern United States (US).

This project can be used by staff and authorized user groups from local, State and Federal agencies to collect new ice jam data, to view the data on an interactive map, and then to prepare those data for submission to the CRREL Ice Jam Archive.

There are web services available for this Ice Jams application. They are documented here: https://test.wim.usgs.gov/icejamsservices/#/
